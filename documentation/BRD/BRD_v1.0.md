# 1. Project Overview

## 1.1 Project Title

**AI-Powered CAT Modelling & Risk Assessment Platform**

---

## 1.2 Background

Global Re Insurance Solutions (GRIS) is developing an AI-powered CAT Modelling platform for SecureLife General Insurance Ltd. The company manages a large portfolio of insured residential and commercial properties across India.

The current risk assessment process relies heavily on spreadsheets and manual calculations, making it difficult to estimate catastrophe-related financial losses quickly and accurately.

The proposed system will automate catastrophe risk assessment, financial loss prediction, exposure analysis, and reporting using Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and data analytics.

---

## 1.3 Business Problem

Insurance companies face significant challenges in estimating financial losses caused by natural catastrophes such as floods, earthquakes, cyclones, and wildfires.

The existing manual process results in:

- Slow risk assessment
- Human errors
- Delayed claim estimation
- Inaccurate premium pricing
- Inefficient reinsurance planning
- Lack of centralized catastrophe reporting

These challenges increase financial risk and reduce operational efficiency.

---

## 1.4 Proposed Solution

The proposed AI-Powered CAT Modelling & Risk Assessment Platform will provide a centralized system to:

- Manage insured property data.
- Store catastrophe event information.
- Assess catastrophe exposure.
- Calculate property vulnerability.
- Predict financial losses using Machine Learning.
- Estimate insurance claim amounts.
- Generate catastrophe risk reports.
- Recommend reinsurance coverage.
- Provide interactive business dashboards.

---

## 1.5 Expected Business Benefits

The proposed solution will help the organization to:

- Improve catastrophe risk assessment accuracy.
- Reduce manual effort.
- Support faster business decisions.
- Improve claim planning.
- Enhance reinsurance strategy.
- Increase operational efficiency.
- Improve reporting and analytics.
- Support data-driven decision-making.
